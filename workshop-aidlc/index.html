AI-DLC Adaptive Workflows

Open-source workflow scaffolds that bring AI-DLC principles to life

Transform your development process with adaptive workflows that intelligently select stages, modulate depth, and embed human oversight at every critical decision point.

Key Features:
  • Adaptive Decisioning: Workflows conform to your problem's shape
  • Transparent Checkpoints: Human approvals embedded at every decision gate
  • End-to-End Traceability: Complete audit trail of reasoning and decisions
Workspace Detection

AI-DLC workflows automatically analyze your workspace and adapt the workflow. For greenfield projects, it proceeds to Requirements Analysis. For brownfield projects, it performs Reverse Engineering first. This demonstrates the truly adaptive nature of AI-DLC workflows.
